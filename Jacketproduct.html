Product Information

Ludo is a very light 3-layer shell jacket with low weight and minimal packing volume. Long ventilation zippers under the sleeves and front pockets ensure optimal breathability and give you the opportunity to regulate the temperature depending on activity level. The hood can be rolled into the collar and offers protection against rain and wind. Perfect for an active outdoor life in rough weather.
