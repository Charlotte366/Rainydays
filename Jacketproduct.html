Productinformation

Ludo is a very light 3-layer shell jacket low weight and minimal packing volume. Long ventilation zippers under the sleeves and front pockets that ensure optimal breathability and give you the opportunity to regulate the temperature depending on the activity level. Ludo has a hood that can be rolled up and becomes part of the collar and gives you better protection against rain and wind. The jacket is well suited for an active and versatile outdoor life in rough weather.
